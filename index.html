Millions of coffee lovers around the world use the most diverse ways to brew their favorite beverage. Various Turks and presses, geyser coffee makers and cars, siphons, buffoers and other devices facilitate cooking and make the aroma of coffee more saturated. Today we will talk about kamex for coffee - an amazing invention that has a clear scientific laboratory background. This elementary device allows you to get a delicious and perfectly fragrant drink with a minimum of labor and time expenses. Kemek himself created Peter Schlumb, an American inventor with German roots. It is precisely his origin that explains the scrupulous attitude to the convenience and saving of his own time and money. This lover of coffee had the knowledge of the true chemist, which allowed him to derive the formula of the most perfect drink. As a result, he developed an ingenious apparatus, which was assembled from laboratory utensils and combines the simplest methods of extraction and filtration.
